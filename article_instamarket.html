Market Layout and Customer segmentation with clustering algorithms

Instacart is an American company founded in 2012 that operates a grocery delivery and pick-up service in the United States and Canada.

The company offers its services via a website and mobile app. The service allows customers to order groceries from participating retailers with the shopping being done by a personal shopper. Orders are fulfilled and delivered by a personal shopper, who picks, packs, and delivers the order.

Instamarket orders are available in Kaggle and contain information about which items were present in each order, as well as the market layout: In which aisle the item was found and what department contains each aisle.

With this data, it is possible to analyze the relations between the orders to make business strategies regarding the layout of the hypermarket:

  • Which aisles should be grouped together in the same department?
  • Which departments should be close to one another?
  • For items that had no aisle assignment, to which aisle should they be sent?
  • Which items should be displayed near the cashier counters?
