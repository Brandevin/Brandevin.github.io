Predicting real estate prices with Regression Models

House prices are commonly known to be influenced by the house area, the preservation of the rooms and the overall quality of the construction. However, other not so intuitive factors may play a hand in the pricing of the house. Some of these factors may include the proximity to a railroad, the shape of the property, the material of the road that leads to the house and many others.

This article analyzes the main factors impacting the house prices in the city of Ames in Iowa, where questions such as the ones below are answered:

  • How much an increase in the kitchen area increases the price?
  • If it was possible to increase the quality of a room of the house, which would increase the house price the most?

The main objetctive of the analysis however is to predict the House Prices as accurately as possible using machine learning models. As the data comes from a Kaggle competition, it is also possible to analyze how well our model performs when compared to other people's submissions.
